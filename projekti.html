“Travel makes one modest. You see what a tiny place you occupy in the world”

Bali, Indonesia

Travelers enjoy Bali for its white sand beaches, snorkeling among coral reefs and shipwrecks, the sacred monkey forest and the Tanah Lot Temple. Shown, Ulun Danu Park.

Paris, France

Paris is always a good idea. Rich with food, art and romance, there's so much to see and do. Ride a boat on the Seine or sit in a cafe with a view of the Eiffel Tower, the Arc de Triomphe, or Notre Dame.

Crete, Greece

The island of Crete is rich with archaeological and mythological history that's reflected in its ancient ruins and cultural attractions. The least expensive month to go is August, when it is also hottest.

Cusco, Peru

Once the capital of the Incan empire, high-altitude Cusco is famous for its ruins, stone streets, Qoriacancha palace and the church of Santo Domingo on the Plaza de Armas, the central square.
